This folder contains zip files pertaining to Section 1 of Milestone 3.

memaslap_logs.zip contains the memaslap logs of the 3 clients from the stability trace.

trace.csv.zip is the middleware log produced during the stability trace.

proc_memaslap.zip contains the processed TPS, RT and RT STDev.

threads_busy.zip is the middleware log produced during the “busy threads” benchmark.
